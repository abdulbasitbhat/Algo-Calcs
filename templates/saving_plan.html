Shopping Calculator

This is a Value Shopping Calculator. It provides insights for optimal shopping with a fixed budget

Applications

If a person has a fixed budget and wants to buy things in order to maximize value of purchase.

If a person has a monthly income and wants to have a fixed amount of savings, he can put budget as (income - savings)

How to Use

Enter data in format - Budget, [Priority list], [Respective Cost list]. Priority must be unique and higher the priority higher should be the number

Example

Alex has to buy 5 items, but has a budget of Rs 40000. He has a priority of all orders.

# Item Cost Priority
1 T.V 7000 2
2 Fridge 10000 10
3 A.C 20000 10
4 Carpet 15000 3
5 Nike Shoes 3000 1

He would input his data as 40000,[2 10 10 3 1],[7000 10000 20000 15000 3000]
